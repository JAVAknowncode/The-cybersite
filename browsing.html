Explanation

Safe browsing means using secure websites and careful habits to avoid faulty downloads, fake logins, and trackers behind you.

Prevention

  • Use HTTPS websites rather than HTTP ones
  • Keep your browser updated
  • Dont download files from untrusted sources
  • Use privacy extensions to block trackers
  • Check URLs carefully before logging into any website
